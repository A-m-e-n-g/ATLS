Radwimps

Radwimps is a prominent Japanese rock band known for their eclectic blend of alternative rock, indie, and occasionally experimental music styles. Formed in 2001 in Kanagawa Prefecture, the band consists of Yojiro Noda (lead vocals, guitar), Akira Kuwahara (guitar), Yusuke Takeda (bass), and Satoshi Yamaguchi (drums). They gained significant popularity both in Japan and internationally.
